Dynamic linkage version of the Tonk library:

Link to the 32-bit or 64-bit version based on your application.
Debug versions may be substituted in to perform additional checks.

x86/tonk.dll
x86/tonk_debug.dll

x86_64/tonk.dll
x86_64/tonk_debug.dll

Methods to automatically load the correct DLL based on 32/64-bit process
are demonstrated in the SDK tests.
